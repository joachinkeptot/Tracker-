## Tips & Tricks

### Analyze Growth

1. Select "Last Quarter"
2. Look at % change in People card (green = good)
3. Check "People Added Over Time" chart
4. Review insights section

### Find Problem Areas

1. View Heat Map
2. Look for areas with light/empty cells
3. Read insights about inactive areas

### Track Learning Progress

1. Select relevant time period
2. Check Learning card for completion counts
3. Compare with "Learning Completions" chart

### Export for Reports

1. Adjust time period to desired range
2. Review all visualizations
3. Click Export button
4. Use PDF in presentations/reports
